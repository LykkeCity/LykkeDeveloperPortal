What is ChronoBank (TIME)?

The ChronoBank team, led by CEO Sergei Sergienko, has come up with a revolutionary idea for implementing blockchain technology in our everyday lives by identifying a valuable resource that everyone around the globe has equal access to, irrespective of geography or the economy. This resource is, quite simply, work, or the effort than a person can invest into a worthwhile endeavor. Consequently, the ChronoBank Foundation has developed the idea of creating a cryptocurrency based on the labor hour.

From this insight has come ChronoBank TIME and LH tokens, which are allocated to people for performing tasks. To date, the ChronoBank Foundation has focused on a few specific industries, such as cleaning, warehousing, E-commerce, construction, and manufacturing. Their ICO was so successful that they collected $5.4 million in a variety of cryptocurrencies, including bitcoin, ether, litecoin, NEM, WAVES, and EMC, in addition to funds in USD.

Their goal is for their tokens to become the standard value measurement in the labor industry worldwide, considering local currency exchange rates, the local value of the labor hour, and other economic factors.

Ultimately, disparities in labor value around the world would disappear, and all workers would earn in direct proportion to their skill or efficiency.

The implementation of the ChronoBank project will occur in two stages. The first is the creation of the TIME and LH crypto tokens. It is interesting to note that the LH token, which represents the value of a labor hour, would not be subject to inflation, since it is backed by actual work hours.

The second stage is the development of LaborX, a decentralised marketplace for employers to allocate ChronoBank tokens to workers in exchange for work provided. This marketplace will eliminate the substantial commission fees assessed by middlemen, and it will enable workers to offer their time to any potential employer.

ChronoBank partners with Lykke

WLykke Corporation, in partnership with ChronoBank, has agreed to list TIME and LH tokens on the Lykke Exchange. This means that, instead of being paid in your local currency by your employer, you could be paid in ChronoBank LH, representing the fair value of your labor, which you could instantly redeem for the fiat currency of your choice, without incurring any transaction fees. It might sound like science fiction, but it is available today on the Lykke Exchange.

Furthermore, Lykke is contributing to the development of LaborX, the second stage of the ChronoBank project.

According to the CEO of ChronoBank, the partnership with Lykke has been exceedingly worthwhile, since both companies share a strong commitment to making cryptocurrencies more accessible to new users.
