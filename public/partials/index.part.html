Build with Lykke

At Lykke, open-source is part of our DNA. All of our projects and tools are available on our GitHub, and we encourage our community to make use of everything we've created.

We also provide API access to many of our core products and services, so you can build charts, add a token to our exchange, or create tools for high-frequency trading.

Prefer to apply your skills to projects already in process? Lykke Streams, our collaboration platform, has a full list of initiatives and bounties.

Questions? Comments? Visit our dedicated developer Telegram channel.
