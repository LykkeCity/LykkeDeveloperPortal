Frequently Asked Questions

When is the right time to buy?

Since owning SLR represents solar electricity generation, there is no good or bad time to purchase it. The sooner you do, the sooner you contribute to this movement. It is in our global interest to utilize the unrealized potential of solar power. A group of volunteers called the SolarCoin Foundation has started this incentive, and Lykke contributes to the distribution of SLR by listing it on our platform, so you can buy it 24/7.

What are the risks with buying SolarCoin (SLR)?

As long as you obtain your SolarCoin from a reliable source, there are no greater risks involved in buying this coin than with any other crypto asset. So, the important question is where to buy Solar Coin. Purchase it at Lykke and store your private key in at least one secure place, if not two or three. Then you completely eliminate the major risk involved in crypto assets: losing them.

Can you sell SolarCoin?

You certainly can sell your SLR. Within your Lykke Wallet, you select a pair, such as SLR/USD, SLR/BTC, SLR/CHF, SLR/JPY, and SLR/GBP, and enter the amount you wish to sell.

Can you buy a partial SolarCoin?

Sure, you can purchase a fraction of a SolarCoin. You decide the purchase method and the amount to spend. The amount of SolarCoin you receive appears on the screen of your mobile device.

What is the best payment method to use when buying SLR?

There are different methods, such as purchasing SLR via bank transfer or credit card, or exchanging SLR for your existing crypto assets. If you complete the transaction within your Lykke Wallet app, you can be confident in the safety of any of these methods. Therefore, we recommend that you choose your favorite.
